## 3. Medical Databases & Guidelines (Interactions)

Factual medical information, drug interactions, and natural coagulation properties are synthesized and mapped to structured formats from clinical reference works and official medical monographs:

* **FDA Prescribing Information:** Official US Food and Drug Administration drug labels (e.g. Coumadin/Warfarin) — Public Domain.
* **Stockley's Drug Interactions:** Clinical drug-drug and food-drug interaction handbook.
* **Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (MSKCC) "About Herbs" Database.**
* **NIH National Center for Complementary and Integrative Health (NCCIH).**

*Note: Factual medical and scientific data are not subject to copyright. The structured JSON schemas and synthesized recommendation keys are custom creations of this repository, released under the MIT License.*
